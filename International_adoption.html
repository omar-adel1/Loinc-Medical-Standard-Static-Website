International Adoption

LOINC is more than a database; it's a developing worldwide community. Although LOINC is based in Indiana, USA, its users are from all around the globe! LOINC is used to share and aggregate health data by large reference laboratories, healthcare organizations, software suppliers, government agencies, NGOs, insurance firms, in vitro diagnostic manufacturers, ministries of health, regional health information networks, national standards bodies, and others.


Translations enable international adoption

To fulfil the demands of various countries, LOINC content and documentation have been translated into a variety of languages. Both RELMA and search.loinc.org feature multilingual searching capabilities, which Regenstrief has created to make the process more efficient for our translators by exploiting the atomic pieces that make up a LOINC phrase.
