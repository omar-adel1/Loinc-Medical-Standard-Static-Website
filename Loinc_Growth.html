Loinc Growth

Users continue to seek additional material in many fields, and LOINC adopts a community-driven development model. New lab tests are constantly being developed in the diagnostic testing sector. Many inquiries now originate from test manufacturers directly. Codes for urgent concerns, monitoring, and case reports are still being requested by public health. Inherited human illnesses, somatic mutations (cancer), and the existence of infectious organisms all have genetic testing. In the previous decade, we've quadrupled the amount of molecular pathology words in LOINC and contributed to the development of new data models for presenting genetic test findings at Health Level 7 (HL7). Social determinants of health, patient-reported outcomes, clinical document titles, and organized pathology reporting for cancer are all expanding fields.
